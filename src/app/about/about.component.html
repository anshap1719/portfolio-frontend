Introduction

Hi, I'm a Student, a Freelancer, a Consultant and a Traveller from Nagpur, India.

I am doing major in Electronics and Communications (Yeah, I know how strange this is my interest being in Computer Science. But, that's a story for another time) from an Engineering College here in Nagpur.




Before I got into development and coding, I was a full-time gamer.

But that all changed when I was tasked to learn basic web development (Simple HTML/CSS) to build a website for an NGO I was in at the time. This was back in 2015.




And I haven't looked back ever since.

This is more evident from the fact that I got a Macbook soon after, leaving my gaming life behind.




Now, I work as a Freelance Full-Stack Developer.

Upwork being my primary source of work, I have worked on a wide range of projects. I also accept a few jobs that I get from connections of my friends and family.
